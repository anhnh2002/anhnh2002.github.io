My current main research interests lie in AI for Software Development (AI4Code, AI4Software), as well as Large Language Models and Agentic Systems. I'm currently conducting research under the guidance of Prof. Bach Le and Dr. Nghi D. Q. Bui at FPT Software AI Center. I graduated early with an excellent Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Hanoi University of Science and Technology. My research focuses on developing algorithms to train and fine-tune Large Language Models for code, and exploring agentic systems for Software Engineering tasks.

Positions: I am currently looking for PhD opportunities in Computer Science for the next academic cycle, with research interests in Software Systems and/or Machine Learning (such as Code LLMs, Software Agents, AI+Systems).

News

  • May 2026: Released RustPrint, a documentation-guided agentic framework for migrating large-scale C codebases to Rust while preserving architectural intent.
  • Apr 2026: Our paper "CodeWiki" has been accepted at Findings of ACL 2026!
  • Jan 2026: Converged has been awarded First Prize at AI for Real Action 2025 - the first national AI competition broadcast on Vietnamese television! This year's challenge focused on building a Vietnamese-native language model that balances capability (Tài) and ethics (Đức) for real-world application in high school education. Our product, ConnectED (Connect Education), enables teachers to generate visual simulations and educational videos in minutes from simple descriptions. Special thanks to teammates Tịnh Lương Sơn and Thang Doan!
  • Oct 2025: Released CodeWiki, an open-source framework for AI-powered repository-level documentation across 7 programming languages.
